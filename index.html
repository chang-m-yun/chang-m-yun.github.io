Bio

I am a PhD candidate at Stanford University in Chemical Engineering, advised by Anshul Kundaje in Computer Science & Genetics. My current research focuses on engineering molecular-scale biology of proteins and DNA using deep learning methods.

For undergraduate, I studied Chemical Engineering at Columbia University, with research in electrocatalytic reduction of CO2 using non-toxic, earth-abundant metals. For graduate school, I studied Biotechnology at the University of Cambridge, with research in engineering bacterial endospores for DNA data storage.
